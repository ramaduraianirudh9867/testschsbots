About SCHS Raiderbots

Who are we?

​The Silver Creek High School Robotics Team, Raiderbots, is a student-managed team that competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition. This team was founded in 2018, and ever since, the team has worked on designing and building their robot, as well as writing their own software.
