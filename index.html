SCHS Raiderbots

Welcome to the Silver Creek High School Robotics Team Website!

Silver Creek High School Robotics is a FIRST Robotics Team founded in 2018. The SCHS Raiderbots, also known as FTC Team 15453, are a dedicated group of students passionate about technology and design. We are supported by a community of mentors, teachers, and parents committed to the success of our fledgling team as we begin our FTC journey. Join us at our first ever competition on February 2nd­­ in Daly City!

Our Mission

​​SCHS Raiderbots, the official Robotics Team of Silver Creek High School, is a student-led organization that enables students to explore STEM pathways through hands-on mechanical and software design and development. Guided by our amazing coaches and mentors, we learn and practice engineering principles and techniques over the course of designing, building, and programming our competition-ready robot, Riley (check out our gallery to see Riley and our team in action). Founded on principles of dedication, innovation, and collaboration, our team is open to all Silver Creek High School Students, regardless of prior experience.

Silver Creek High School

Founded in 1969 in San Jose, Silver Creek High School is a California Distinguished School and the home of the SCHS Raiderbots. As a student-run club, our team embraces Silver Creek High's mission to provide all students with equal access to quality education and opportunity in a supportive, encouraging environment. To learn more about Silver Creek High School, visit our homepage at schs.schoolloop.com.
